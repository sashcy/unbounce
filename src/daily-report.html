About the author

The daily reports are written by Dave Evans, market analyst and financial news editor.

Dave’s background is in psychology and previously practiced as a psychotherapist. He believes that financial markets can show bias in certain trading scenarios similarly to how individuals can exhibit mental bias when it comes to money.

His daily reports are a blend of fundamental, political, and technical analyses of the Forex markets, with a primarily quantitative approach to trading stock markets.

Dave’s commentaries have appeared in various media including City A.M. and the Dow Jones Newswires.
